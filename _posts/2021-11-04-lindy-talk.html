Lindy Talk

I've recently had the opportunity to discuss my research in math oncology on the podcast called LindyTalk.

My conversation with Paul Skallas, AKA the Lindyman, spanned the breadth of evolution & ecology in cancer. The podcast is a more of a generalist podcast (not specific to cancer), so our conversation focused more layman's understanding of evolution, and how evolutionary principles can be useful in designing cancer treatment. I also mention antifragility and future work that we are doing to design antifragile cancer therapy.
